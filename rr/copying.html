Copying and Printing Services for Collection Materials at the Library of Congress

Back to Researchers Home

The Library provides photocopying and printing services in most, but not all reading rooms.  The chart below lists this information by reading room.  Researchers may pay for prints with a credit card or a copy card purchased with cash from one of five vending kiosks. The copy card vending kiosks accept denominations of $1, $5, $10, and $20, and there is no fee for the purchase of a copy card.  The minimum card purchase is $1.  Receipts are provided by the vending kiosks, which are located in the Jefferson Building (Main Reading Room); Madison Building (Lobby, Law Library, and Newspaper Reading Room); and Adams Building (Business and Science reading room). 

The Library’s printing fees are as follows:

  • Black-and-white printed pages (8.5 x 11 or 8.5 x 14, single-sided) – 20 cents per page
  • Black-and-white printed pages (11 x 17) – 40 cents per page
  • Color printed pages (8.5 x 11 or 8.5 x 14) – 50 cents per page
  • Color printed pages (11 x 17) – $1.00 per page
  • Color scans to email – no charge

Subject to copyright and rights restrictions, patron-supplied flash drives may also generally be used to save images from computers at no charge.
