FORMAT CHANGE LIST

Update No. 10, October 2009 and Update No. 11, 2010
There are no changes to the MARC 21 Format for Classification Data for Update No. 10, October 2009, and Update No. 11, February 2010, resulting from proposals that were considered by the MARC 21 community during 2009 and January 2010.
