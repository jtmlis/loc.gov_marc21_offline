MARC 21 Holdings
October 2007

MARC 21 FORMAT FOR HOLDINGS DATA

FORMAT CHANGE LIST

Update No. 8, October 2007
This page documents the changes contained in the eighth update to the 1999 edition of the MARC 21 Format for Holdings Data. Update No. 8 (October 2007) includes changes resulting from proposals that were considered by the MARC 21 community during 2007.
