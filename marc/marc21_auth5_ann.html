Update No. 5
MARC 21 Format for Authority Data (1999 Edition)

Network Development and MARC Standards Office
Library of Congress

Update No. 5 (October 2004) to the MARC 21 Format for Authority Data is now available from the Library of Congress. It includes changes made to the authority format resulting from proposals which were considered by the ALA ALCTS/LITA/RUSA Machine-Readable Bibliographic Information Committee (MARBI) at its 2004 meetings, the Canadian Committee on MARC (CCM) at its meetings in 2004 and by the Book Industry Communications/Bibliographic Standards Technical Subgroup (BIC/BSTS) at its 2004 meetings. This update features important new information including:

  • Addition of field 031 (Musical Incipits Information)
  • Updated examples
  • Improved descriptions of content designators
