MARC 21 Authority
April 2012

This section is provided for the lists of format changes that accompany each update to the MARC 21 Format for Authority Data.

MARC 21 FORMAT FOR AUTHORITY DATA

FORMAT CHANGE LIST

Update No. 14, April 2012
This page documents the changes contained in the fourteenth update to the 1999 edition of the MARC 21 Format for Authority Data. Update No. 14 (Update 2012) includes changes resulting from proposals that were considered by the MARC 21 community during January and June 2012.
