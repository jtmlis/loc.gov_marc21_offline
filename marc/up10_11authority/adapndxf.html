Appendix F - Format Changes

MARC 21 Authority
October 2009/February 2010

This section is provided for the lists of format changes that accompany each update to the MARC 21 Format for Authority Data.

MARC 21 FORMAT FOR AUTHORITY DATA

FORMAT CHANGE LIST

Update No. 10, October 2009 and Update No. 11, February 2010
This page documents the changes contained in the tenth and eleventh update to the 1999 edition of the MARC 21 Format for Authority Data. Update No. 10 (October 2009) and Update No. 11 (February 2010) include changes resulting from proposals that were considered by the MARC 21 community during 2009 and January 2010.
