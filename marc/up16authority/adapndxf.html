Appendix F - Format Changes

MARC 21 Authority
April 2013

This section is provided for the lists of format changes that accompany each update to the MARC 21 Format for Authority Data.

MARC 21 FORMAT FOR AUTHORITY DATA

FORMAT CHANGE LIST

Update No. 16, April 2013
This page documents the changes contained in the sixteenth update to the 1999 edition of the MARC 21 Format for Authority Data. Update No. 16 (April 2013) includes changes resulting from proposals that were considered by the MARC 21 community during January 2013.
UPDATE 16 CHANGES
■ New content designators:
Subfields
$o Single or starting date for aggregated content (NR) in 046 (Special Coded Dates)
$p Ending date for aggregated content (NR) in 046 (Special Coded Dates)
Fields
X62 Medium of Performance Terms - General Information
162 Heading - Medium of Performance Term (NR)
385 Audience Characteristics (R)
386 Creator/Contributor Characteristics (R)
462 See From Tracing - Medium of Performance Term (R)
562 See Also From Tracing - Medium of Performance Term (R)
672 Title Related to the Entity (R)
673 Title Not Related to the Entity (R)
762 Established Heading Linking Entry - Medium of Performance Term (R)
