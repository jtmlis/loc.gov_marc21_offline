TECHNICAL NOTICE (May 31, 2013) -- revised June 27, 2013

Additions to the MARC Code List for Relators

Network Development and MARC Standards Office
Library of Congress

The codes listed below have been added to the MARC Code List for Relators and to the Library of Congress Linked Data Service.

The codes should not be used in exchange records until 30 days after the date of this notice to provide implementers time to include newly-defined codes in any validation tables.

  • Additions:
    • abr - Abridger
    • apl - Appellant
    • ape - Appellee
    • adi - Art director
    • ato - Autographer
    • brl - Braille embosser
    • brd - Broadcaster
    • cas - Caster
    • cor - Collection registrar
    • cou - Court-governed
    • crt - Court reporter
    • edc - Editor of compilation
    • edm - Editor of moving image work
    • enj - Enacting jurisdiction
    • fds - Film distributor
    • fmk - Filmmaker [code announced May 31, 2013 was “fml”]
    • fmd - Film director
    • fmp - Film producer
    • his - Host institution [code announced May 31, 2013 was “hsi”]
    • isb - Issuing body
    • jud - Judge
    • jug - Jurisdiction governed
    • osp - On-screen presenter
    • pan - Panelist
    • pra - Praeses
    • pre - Presenter
    • prn - Production company
    • prs - Production designer
    • rdd - Radio director
    • rpc - Radio producer
    • rsr - Restorationist
    • sll - Seller
    • sgd - Stage director
    • tld - Television director
    • tlp - Television producer
    • wac - Writer of added commentary
    • wat - Writer of added text
    • wal - Writer of added lyrics
  • Deprecated relators:
    • clb - Collaborator
      (Combined with “Contributor”)
    • voc - Vocalist
      (Combined with “Singer” (added as reference))
