MARC 21 FORMAT FOR COMMUNITY INFORMATION

Update No. 13, September 2011
This page documents the changes contained in the thirteenth update to the 1999 edition of the MARC 21 Format for Community Information. Update No. 13 (September 2011) includes changes resulting from proposals that were considered by the MARC 21 community during January and June 2011.
