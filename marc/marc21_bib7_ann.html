MARC: News and Announcements (May 14, 2007)

Update No. 7

MARC 21 Format for Bibliographic Data

Network Development and MARC Standards Office
Library of Congress

Update No. 7 (October 2006) to the MARC 21 Format for Bibliographic Data is now available from the Library of Congress. It includes changes made to the bibliographic format resulting from proposals which were considered by the ALA ALCTS/LITA/RUSA Machine-Readable Bibliographic Information Committee (MARBI) at its 2006 meetings, the Canadian Committee on MARC (CCM) at its meetings in 2006, and by the BIC Bibliographic Standards Group (BIC/BSG) at its 2006 meetings. This update features important new information including:

  • Addition of subfield $j (Relator term) in fields 111, 611, 711 and 811
  • Addition of subfield $u (Uniform Resource Identifier) in field 852

Update No. 7 to the MARC 21 Format for Bibliographic Data is available for $15 worldwide (Except in Canada -- See below for ordering information in Canada).
